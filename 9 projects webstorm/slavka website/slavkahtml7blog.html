Poem of the Stones

06/07/2012

Recognition comes when we find our self at the crossroad

That guides us to pure faith

The true wisdom is a word of victory

The gently action give us happiness of gain

Govern with the hart is the passion without and

Only free king can make a new decision

Wholesome surprises lead us to easy compassion

Entire renaissance come from a seed that contain intention

Where a deep circumstance contains fundamental innocence

The energy of the sun has instinct to build trace

The life itself creates a netting of harmony
